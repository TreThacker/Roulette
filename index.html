Bets Explained:

Straight Number Bet
Place your chip directly on one number on the main betting grid. This covers only that exact number. Pays 36:1.
Example: 10 chips on #17 pays 360 chips if 17 hits.

Split Bet
Place your chip on the line between two touching numbers. This covers both numbers. Pays 18:1.
Example: 10 chips on a 17/18 split pays 180 chips if either 17 or 18 hits.

Corner Bet
Place your chip where four numbers meet at an intersection. This covers all four numbers. Pays 9:1.
Example: 10 chips on 1/2/4/5 pays 90 chips if any of those four numbers hit.

Red Bet
Place your chip on the Red side bet on the left side of the grid. This covers all green-colored numbers. Pays 2:1.
Example: 10 chips on Red pays 20 chips if a green number hits.

Black Bet
Place your chip on the Black side bet on the left side of the grid. This covers all blue-colored numbers. Pays 2:1.
Example: 10 chips on Black pays 20 chips if a blue number hits.

Odd / Even Bet
Place your chip on Odd or Even on the left side of the grid. Zero does not count as odd or even. Pays 2:1.
Example: 10 chips on Even pays 20 chips if any even number from 2 through 36 hits.

Low / High Bet
Place your chip on 1-18 for low numbers or 19-36 for high numbers. Zero does not count. Pays 2:1.
Example: 10 chips on 1-18 pays 20 chips if any number from 1 through 18 hits.

Dozen Bet
Place your chip on 1st 12, 2nd 12, or 3rd 12. 1st 12 covers 1-12, 2nd 12 covers 13-24, and 3rd 12 covers 25-36. Pays 3:1.
Example: 10 chips on 2nd 12 pays 30 chips if any number from 13 through 24 hits.

Column Bet
Place your chip on one of the 2:1 boxes at the bottom of the number columns. This covers all numbers in that vertical column. Pays 3:1.
Example: 10 chips on a column bet pays 30 chips if any number in that column hits.
